Welcome to another Triage Thursday! This is where we share all the new features and updates we’ve added to our Triage sandbox over the past week. If you have any thoughts or feedback, we’d love to hear from you! Feel free to reach out to us anytime, we’re always here to help.

Let’s get straight into what’s new this week:

New Families This Week

  • Added extraction support for Pikabot V2 family
  • Added extraction support for Socks5Systemz
  • Added detection for Xamalicious Android backdoor family
  • Added detection for Qakbot v5 x64bit variant

Updates for Existing Families

  • Updated detection for Fabookie unextracted samples
  • Updated detection for Gootloader undetected samples

If you have any feedback, questions, or issues about Triage feel free to reach out to us any time - we do our best to respond to all feedback but even if we can’t get back to you straight away your files will go into our list of things to review and help us prioritize tasks.

As always feel free to reach out to us any time directly through the website, or using the Feedback option on an analysis report page.
