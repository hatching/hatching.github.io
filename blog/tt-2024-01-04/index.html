Happy New Year and welcome to Triage Thursday! It’s always a pleasure to have you here with us. This week marks our very first week of 2024, and we hope you’re doing great and are ready to start the new year with us. If you’re new to this blog series, no worries! This is where we’ll be sharing all the latest updates we’ve made to the sandbox in the past week. So, sit back, relax and enjoy.

Let’s take a look at what’s new this week.

New Families This Week

  • Added detection and extractor for Spybanker Android banking trojan family
  • Added detection and signature for TrickMo Android banking trojan
  • Added detection for Guerrilla additional plugin payload
  • Added detection for PSWstealer family
  • Updated detection for Vidar family recent version

Updates for Existing Families

  • Updated detection for Pikabot family
  • Updated detection for Socks5Systemz family
  • Fixed Snakebot condition to avoid false positives
